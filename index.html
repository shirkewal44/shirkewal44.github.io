Life to the Limit
I recently read the memoir Life to the Limit, which followed Button's journey from a young boy karting in Somerset to becoming the 2009 Formula 1 World Champion. More than a racing story, the memoir is an honest account of what it truly costs to dedicate your entire life to a single dream. It made me think about ambition, sacrifice, and the people who carry the weight of our dreams alongside us.
